Alfred Nobel

A man beween inventions of destruction and peace

      1833 21th october born in Stockholm
      1842 He moves to Saint Petersburg, where his father founds several ironworks
      1850 While studying he met Ascanio Sobrero in Paris. The discoverer of nitroglycerine
      1860 Beginning of experiments with explosives in mining
      1863 Development of the initial ignition for the safe ignition of explosives
      1865 First mass production of nitroglycerin accompanied by several serious accidents
      1867 Patent a safe nitroglycerin explosive in Great Britain, Sweden and the USA. He calls it dynamite
      1875 Development of the Blasting gelatin
      1887 Patent for Ballestit, which revolutionized weapon technology from pistol to cannon
      1888 After an erroneous obituary in a french newspaper with the title "the merchant of death is dead" he turns his gaze how the world will see him after his death
      1895 He determined in his will to give the largest part of his fortune to the foundation witch will later be known as "Nobel-price foundation"
      1896 At the 10. of December, Alfred dies of brain hemorrhage in Sanremo
      1900 The official founding of the Nobel-price foundation takes place
      1901 At the 5th anniversary of Albert Nobel's death, the first awarding of the Nobelprice takes place. It goes to Henry Dunant and Frederic Passy
